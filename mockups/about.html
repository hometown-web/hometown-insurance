Rooted in Our Community

Welcome to Hometown Insurance, one of New York State’s leading independent insurance agencies. Proudly serving individuals, families, and businesses since 1979, we provide customized home insurance, auto insurance, business insurance, and specialty coverage solutions throughout Long Island and New York State.


While our agency has grown over the years, our core values remain the same. We continue to uphold the traditions established by our founders and the high standards of service and professionalism that our clients have trusted for generations.


Our formula for success is simple: we treat our neighbors like family. As an independent agency, our team works as your personal insurance advisors—using our experience to help you find the right home, auto, or business insurance coverage at the most competitive price.
