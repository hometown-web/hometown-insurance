General Information

Who is Hometown Insurance Agency?

Hometown Insurance Agency is an independent insurance agency based on Long Island, NY, providing customized insurance solutions for individuals, families, and businesses, including home insurance, auto insurance, business insurance, and specialized programs for Fire and EMS districts.

What types of insurance coverage do I really need?

Your insurance needs depend on factors like your lifestyle, assets, business operations, and potential risks. A personalized review helps ensure you have the right home, auto, or business insurance coverage without paying for protection you don’t need.

How can an insurance advisor help beyond just finding a policy?

An experienced insurance advisor helps identify risks, explain coverage options for home, auto, and business insurance, advocate for you during claims, and adjust your policies as your life or business evolves.

How often should I review or update my insurance policies?

It’s recommended to review your home, auto, or business insurance policies at least once a year—or anytime you experience a major life or business change such as renovations, new equipment, business growth, or additional drivers or employees.

Fire & EMS Districts

What is LOSAP and do you provide it?

LOSAP (Length of Service Award Program) is a benefit program designed for volunteer firefighters. Hometown Insurance provides expert LOSAP consulting and administration services, with some of the lowest administration fees available in New York State.

Are volunteer members covered under our district auto policy?

Yes. Our specialized Fire and EMS insurance programs often include coverage for members’ personal vehicles while they are responding to calls or performing official duties.

Personal Insurance

What’s the difference between replacement cost and actual cash value?

Replacement cost covers what it takes to replace an item today, which is common in many home insurance policies, while actual cash value factors in depreciation. Understanding the difference can significantly impact how much you receive after a claim.

Can I bundle policies to save money?

Yes. Bundling home insurance and auto insurance policies is one of the most common ways to save money, and it can also simplify managing your insurance coverage.

What factors affect my insurance premiums?

Insurance premiums for home, auto, and business insurance may be influenced by several factors, including:

  • Location
  • Claims history
  • Coverage limits
  • Industry type
  • Property value
  • Safety measures
  • Credit history (where permitted)

Do I need umbrella or excess liability coverage?

If you have significant assets, rental properties, or a business, umbrella liability coverage provides an additional layer of protection beyond your standard home or auto insurance policy limits.

What does my policy not cover?

Exclusions vary depending on whether it’s a home, auto, or business insurance policy, but common gaps may include floods, earthquakes, cyber incidents, or certain high-value items. Reviewing your policies with an advisor can help identify and close these gaps.

Claims Process

How do I file a claim, and what should I expect?

Claims for home insurance, auto insurance, or business insurance can usually be filed online or by phone with your insurance carrier. An adjuster will review the loss, gather documentation, and guide you through the process until the claim is resolved.

Billing & Payments

How do deductibles work, and how do I choose the right one?

A deductible is the amount you pay out of pocket before your home, auto, or business insurance coverage takes effect. Higher deductibles typically mean lower premiums, but it’s important to choose an amount you’re comfortable paying if a claim occurs.
